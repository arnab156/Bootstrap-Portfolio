MadeofChicago™ Purple Line Collection

Inspired from the Cloud Gate and Pantone Color of the year. Cloud gate is inspired from the curves and mercury-like shine of the Could Gate. The Could Gate was designed by an Indian-born British sculptor, Anish Kapoor. I think I find it personally motivating that Indians and immigrants have had huge impact on the design and arts of the city.

MadeofChicago™ Tower Black Collection

Inspired from the famous towers of Chicago. This collection is made with 4-way high stretch sweat-wicking fabric highlighting the strength and resillence of the city. Chicago's beloved tower such as the 875 North Michigan Avenue (popularly known as Hancock Tower) and the Willis Tower (also known as Sears Tower) were work of art by architect, Fazlur Rahman Khan, a Bengali-American immiogrant , just like me!
