About me

Arnab Majumdar is an enthusiactic individual. He has more than a decade of experience in the corporate industry, academia as well as in a start-up. Arnab has a Masters degree in Textiles, Fashion Merchandising and Design from the University of Rhode Island. Arnab acquired his undergraduate degree at National Institute of Fashion Technology, India.

Arnab is currently Assistant Professor at Northwood University and also owns a Chicago based small business called Made of Chicago ™. Made of Chicago is a Chicago designed and Chicago inspired activewear clothing brand selling that primarily sells in a custom-made 55" LED vending machines. Arnab aspires to find a position as a web developer and to accomplish that he is attending Northwestern Coding Bootcamp™ to acquire teschnical skills needed in Chicago's market. Arnab's enjoys cooking, gardening, tennis, ping-pong, painting, and technology. Whenever he can Arnab volunters at Lincoln Park Conservatory as a Docent.
